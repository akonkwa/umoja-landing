PAMOJA / Concept Rewrite / March 2026

PAMOJA

Agentic network intelligence for understanding the past, navigating the present, and shaping the future.

Pamoja is a personal relationship intelligence system that connects signals across LinkedIn, Instagram, Gmail, calendars, events, and other networked platforms to build a living picture of a user's social and professional world. Instead of leaving relationships scattered across apps, Pamoja creates a persistent agent that remembers, interprets, and recommends.

Core Questions

Past What shaped my network over time?
Present What is happening in my network right now?
Future What should I do next, and who should I meet?
P

Past View

Pamoja reconstructs the history of a user's network by identifying major phases, institutions, roles, communities, and recurring themes. It highlights the people and moments that helped shape the current network and makes long-term patterns visible.

N

Present View

The system shows active relationships, weak ties worth revisiting, overlapping communities, and people aligned with current goals. It turns fragmented communication activity into an interpretable map of who matters now.

F

Future View

Pamoja recommends introductions, follow-ups, events, and strategic moves. It can identify two people who should meet, surface a timely dormant relationship, or suggest a conference that fits both the user's goals and their network pathways.

What Makes It Agentic

A normal app stores contact data. Pamoja reasons across time, keeps memory about relationships, and proposes actions based on patterns, relevance, and user goals.

System Components

  • A persistent Personal Network Agent for each user
  • Source connectors for LinkedIn, Instagram, Gmail, calendars, and events
  • A memory layer for entities, timelines, themes, and relationship history
  • A recommendation layer for intros, follow-ups, and opportunity discovery
  • A visualization layer for past, present, and future views

Initial MVP Focus

The first version should be deliberately narrow: a user-authorized LinkedIn analysis experience that creates a visual representation of the user's past network.

  • Connect LinkedIn through a secure, consent-based workflow
  • Retrieve permitted profile, role, education, and activity signals
  • Generate a timeline-based map of the user's network history
  • Highlight key institutions, transitions, clusters, and bridges

Example Outputs

  • "These two contacts should meet because they work on adjacent problems in different communities."
  • "This relationship is dormant but highly relevant to your current direction."
  • "This conference fits your goals and overlaps strongly with your extended network."
  • "Your network has shifted from one domain to another, and here are the people who drove that change."
